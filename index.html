About audio capture / Spotify

Listen to Tab opens your browser’s sharing picker. To react to Spotify Web Player, choose its browser tab and enable Share tab audio. A webpage cannot silently inspect another tab’s sound. Host this on GitHub Pages or localhost for the most reliable permission behavior; the sculpture itself runs offline either way.
